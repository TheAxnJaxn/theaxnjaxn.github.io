About Me

I've been interested in software development ever since I taught myself to build static web pages in middle school and took a C++ high school course to create a text-based adventure game. I love a challenge and learning new things every day. After working in online marketing, I missed coding interactive programs that are useful to other people.

I completed an immersive, full-stack web development program to gain hands-on experience developing web applications from design through deployment. Afterward, I worked for an e-commerce company to deploy applications and APIs that allowed people to create amazing print projects.

I'm a supportive teammate with strong cross-team communication skills. I've worked on green-field development, bug-fixing, and automated test code coverage. Outside of the office, I'm a voracious bookworm, I volunteer my time to nonprofits, and I enjoy the fresh air on a long hike or motorcycle trip.

Experience

Senior Software Engineer on the Platform team at Blurb Inc.

The Platform Engineering team was primarily responsible for backend architecture to make all this possible and often worked on the full stack to develop new features.

I was an integral contributor when we created B2B APIs to open our print on demand services to partners like StoryWorth, expanded from printing books to new custom wall art product types, and created Java microservices from portions of functionality pulled out of our monolithic Ruby on Rails application.

I also expanded e-commerce functionality in our Ruby on Rails application with features like unapplying coupon codes, expanding auto-applied bulk discounts to be product-specific, and giving internal admin users greater functionality through admin tools.
