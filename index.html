About Me

I've been interested in software development ever since I taught myself to build static web pages in middle school and took a C++ high school course to create a text-based adventure game. I love a challenge and learning new things every day. After working in online marketing, I found myself missing the challenge of building something useful.

I completed an immersive, full-stack web development program to gain hands-on experience developing web applications from design through deployment. Afterward, I worked for an e-commerce company to deploy applications and APIs that enable anyone to self-publish books, magazines and ebooks. I was an integral contributor when we: created B2B APIs to open our print on demand services, expanded to new custom print product types, and created Java microservices from portions of functionality pulled out of our monolithic Ruby on Rails application.

I'm a supportive teammate with strong cross-team communication skills. I've worked on green-field development, bug-fixing, and automated test code coverage. Outside of the office, I'm a voracious bookworm, I volunteer my time to nonprofits, and I enjoy the fresh air on a long hike or motorcycle trip.
