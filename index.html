About Me

I've been interested in software development ever since I took a C++ course and later taught myself to build static HTML pages for my family. I love a challenge, and I enjoy learning new things every single day. I have a background in online marketing and e-commerce, but found myself missing the fun of solving challenges through programming.

So I started teaching myself, and then decided to dedicate all of my time to pursuing this new career path. I've recently completed App Academy, an immersive, full-stack web development program in San Francisco, with an acceptance rate below 5%.

I love the problem solving and creativity involved in programming, and I'm eager to further develop my skills. When I'm not coding, I keep active by going to the archery range, enjoying the slopes on my snowboard, or taking in the view along Highway 1 from my motorcycle.
